Welcome to Marble Falls Cemetery Association

The Marble Falls Cemetery Association is a dedicated nonprofit organization committed to the preservation, maintenance, and historical protection of cemetery grounds in Marble Falls, Texas. Our mission is to honor the memories of those who came before us while providing a peaceful and respectful environment for families, visitors, and future generations.

About Our Organization

The Marble Falls Cemetery Association is a registered **501(c)(3) nonprofit organization** devoted to the care, restoration, and preservation of cemetery grounds and historical burial sites in the Marble Falls community. Through donations, volunteer work, and community support, the association maintains the cemetery as a place of remembrance, respect, and heritage.

Our organization works closely with families, historians, volunteers, and local partners to ensure that cemetery spaces remain well maintained and accessible. We believe cemeteries are not only sacred spaces for remembering loved ones, but also important historical landmarks that reflect the cultural and social history of the region.

By supporting the Marble Falls Cemetery Association, community members help ensure that the cemetery remains a place of dignity, respect, and tranquility for generations to come.

Our Services

Cemetery Maintenance

Our volunteers and staff ensure that cemetery grounds remain clean, safe, and well maintained. This includes landscaping, restoration work, and ongoing preservation of headstones and monuments.

Historical Preservation

The cemetery holds valuable historical information about the Marble Falls community. Our association works to preserve these records and protect historically significant grave markers.

Community Support

Families and visitors rely on the cemetery as a place of remembrance and reflection. We assist with general inquiries, cemetery records, and provide guidance related to the cemetery grounds.

Volunteer Programs

Volunteers play a critical role in our mission. From helping with cleanup days to assisting with historical research, volunteers help preserve the cemetery's legacy.

Our History and Mission

The Marble Falls Cemetery represents generations of local history. Many of the individuals buried here helped build the early community and shape the development of Marble Falls, Texas.

The Marble Falls Cemetery Association was created to ensure that this historic cemetery continues to be maintained with care and respect. Our mission is to protect the integrity of burial sites while honoring the legacy of those laid to rest here.

Through responsible stewardship and community involvement, the association strives to preserve this sacred place as a lasting historical landmark for future generations.

Support Our Mission

As a nonprofit organization, the Marble Falls Cemetery Association relies on donations and community support to fund maintenance, restoration projects, landscaping, and historical preservation efforts.

Because we are recognized as a **501(c)(3) nonprofit organization**, donations may be tax-deductible as permitted by law.

Your contribution helps ensure that the cemetery remains a peaceful and respectful place for families, visitors, and the community.

Volunteer Opportunities

Community volunteers are vital to the continued care of the Marble Falls Cemetery. Volunteers may assist with landscaping projects, documentation of historical markers, and general improvement efforts.

Whether you are a local resident, a history enthusiast, or simply someone who wants to help preserve a meaningful place in the community, we welcome your involvement.

Please contact us if you are interested in joining our volunteer efforts.
