Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Founded by passionate community members, Scenic El Dorado Inc was established with a singular vision: to ensure that the natural beauty and historical heritage of El Dorado, Kansas, remain vibrant and accessible for generations to come.

As a registered 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization, our work relies on the generous contributions of donors, the tireless efforts of our volunteers, and strong partnerships with local municipalities. We believe that scenic conservation is not just about planting trees or picking up litter; it is about building a community that takes pride in its surroundings.

From maintaining local parks to organizing massive community clean-ups, our initiatives are designed to bring people together while making a tangible, positive impact on our environment. All donations made to our organization are fully tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Our Initiatives

We take a hands-on approach to environmental stewardship and community beautification. Discover the programs that make Scenic El Dorado Inc a cornerstone of our community.

Landscape Conservation

We actively manage and protect vulnerable green spaces within El Dorado. By removing invasive species and planting native flora, we restore balance to local ecosystems and create beautiful areas for public recreation.

Community Cleanups

Our monthly volunteer drives tackle litter along highways, riverbanks, and public parks. These events are the heartbeat of our organization, bringing together families, schools, and businesses to keep El Dorado pristine.

Educational Outreach

We partner with local schools in the KS 67042 area to teach the next generation about sustainability, recycling, and the importance of native plant life. Knowledge is the seed from which conservation grows.
